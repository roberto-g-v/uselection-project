Conclusions

- We can see that there was a very small correlation between poverty and Votes. Poorest counties such as Clinch and Clay from Georgia State votes lean for Republican party. Just Clay, also from Georgia changed its votes for Biden.

- With unemployment as well we see a small correlation towards a winner. Votes trend is very equate.

- Coronavirus deaths shows no correlation at all as for deciding one party or another.

- Median Income per capita also shows a very equate trend and let us see that highest votes percentage were concentrated in middle income per capita.
